What it checks

Six things your PC is hiding from you

Most PC problems are silent — your machine just gets slower and hotter over time. PCLabs surfaces what Windows never tells you.

System Profile
Captures your full hardware snapshot — CPU, GPU, RAM (total and speed), storage capacity and free space, motherboard, BIOS version and age, display resolution, and refresh rate. Everything in one place, no Device Manager digging.
Storage Health
Checks every physical drive via Windows' built-in health APIs — drive type (SSD or HDD), health status, and operational state. Flags any drive that isn't reporting clean so you can back up before data loss happens.
Stability & Crashes
Parses your Windows Event Log for the last 30 days — BSODs with stop codes, unexpected shutdowns, hardware errors, application crashes, and service failures. Shows the actual apps crashing and how often, ranked by frequency.
Performance
Measures live CPU and RAM usage, disk queue length, startup program count and impact, background process memory consumption, thermal throttling events, and whether your power plan is silently capping your CPU speed.
Windows Health & Security
Checks pending Windows Updates, Defender antivirus status, real-time protection, Windows activation, Secure Boot, TPM state, BitLocker encryption, pending restarts, and any devices reporting errors in Device Manager.
Network & Gaming
Detects your connection type (Ethernet vs Wi-Fi), tests internet connectivity, measures ping latency and packet loss to 8.8.8.8, flags active VPN adapters, and produces an overall gaming readiness score based on your hardware and network.
