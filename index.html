FAQ
Common questions.
Is PCLabs free?

Yes — the beta is completely free to download and use. No account required. A Pro tier may follow after launch, but the core scan will always be free.

Does it fix my PC automatically?

No. PCLabs diagnoses and recommends — it does not make changes on your behalf. Every suggestion comes with an explanation and you decide what to act on. No registry tweaks, no silent changes.

Does it work for gaming?

Yes. PCLabs detects your installed games and provides specific settings recommendations tuned to your hardware. It also estimates FPS ranges and identifies if your CPU, GPU, or RAM is the bottleneck.

Do I need technical experience?

None at all. Every result is explained in plain English. If the scan finds a problem, it tells you what it means and what to do — without assuming you know what a PCIe lane or thermal throttle is.

What does it support?

PCLabs supports Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). Game optimization profiles are available for a growing list of titles. New profiles are added with each update.
